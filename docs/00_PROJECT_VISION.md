## Mission
The Veytec Experience Platform enables organizations to create memorable, repeatable, AI-powered customer and employee experiences that showcase technology through interaction instead of presentation.

## What We Are Building
A reusable platform for branded experiences. The platform will combine camera capture, uploads, AI generation, galleries, live walls, QR flows, prompts, assets, AI Agent interaction, and customer-specific configuration into a repeatable system.

The platform should make it easy to launch new branded experiences quickly through configuration instead of rebuilding custom HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and workflow logic for every new event.

## Why It Matters
Veytec can use this platform to support events, customer kickoffs, internal celebrations, executive demos, trade shows, migration adoption experiences, and customer engagement campaigns without rebuilding each experience from scratch.

The goal is to reduce the time required to launch a new customer-ready experience from weeks to hours, and eventually to minutes.

## Future Platform Capabilities
The platform may include an optional AI Companion powered by Webex AI Agent or Webex Contact Center capabilities.

The AI Companion could allow attendees or customers to ask questions such as:
- How was my image created?
- Can I download this?
- Can I 3D print this?
- How does the experience work?
- How does Webex AI Agent fit into this?
- How does Webex Contact Center support this use case?
- Can I schedule a follow-up conversation?

This turns the experience from a static image gallery into an interactive demonstration of Veytec, Webex AI Agent, Webex Connect, and Webex Contact Center.

## Guiding Principles
1. Configuration over customization.
2. Reusable components first.
3. Security by design.
4. Professional by default.
5. Document as we build.
6. Customer value drives priorities.
7. New experiences should be created quickly without modifying shared platform code whenever possible.
