## Mission
The Veytec Experience Platform enables organizations to create memorable, repeatable, AI-powered customer and employee experiences that showcase technology through interaction instead of presentation.

## What We Are Building
A reusable platform for branded experiences. The platform will combine camera capture, uploads, AI generation, galleries, live walls, QR flows, prompts, assets, and customer-specific configuration into a repeatable system.

## Why It Matters
Veytec can use this platform to support events, customer kickoffs, internal celebrations, executive demos, and migration adoption experiences without rebuilding each experience from scratch.

## Guiding Principles
1. Configuration over customization.
2. Reusable components first.
3. Security by design.
4. Professional by default.
5. Document as we build.
6. Customer value drives priorities.
